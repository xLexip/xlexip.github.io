Third Party Apps / Extentions#

  • Magisk: For root access (recommended)
  • Call Recorder (Magisk Module): Uninstall the updates of the phone app to get it working.
  • Stereo Speaker Mod (Flashable ZIP): Use the earpiece as a second speaker.
  • Swift Backup (root): For extensive app and app data backups.
  • FKM (root): Best kernel toolbox out there.
  • Beta Maniac: Get official beta access to your installed apps.
  • Swift Installer (root): Costumize your dark theme.
  • Volume Styles: Costumize your volume panel.
  • Poweramp Equalizer: A better equalizer.
  • TapTap: Get the double-tap gesture from the early Android 11 DPs.
  • BBS (root): Get detailed battery usage stats.
  • OnePlus Camera + OnePlus Gallery: Get back the OnePlus Cam and Gallery (GCam is recommended)
